# Public website

## Description

A publicly accessible website that may collect visitor data through forms, cookies, or analytics.

## Typical data

- visitor IP addresses
- cookies and device identifiers
- contact form submissions (email, phone, name)
- behavioral analytics data

## Common regulatory triggers

- personal data collection via website
- cookie consent requirements
- privacy policy requirement
- data localization (152-ФЗ)

## Common red flags

- no privacy policy
- no cookie consent banner
- analytics without anonymization
- contact forms without data processing notice

## Recommended controls

- publish privacy policy
- implement cookie consent
- anonymize analytics data
- secure form submissions
- data retention limits
